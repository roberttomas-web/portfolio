Living Spaces Convention

ABOUT

The premier architecture showcase of the year, streaming September 14th on YouTube.

We're thrilled to bring the Living Spaces Convention to a global audience with a free livestream on YouTube — making it easy for architects, designers, and enthusiasts everywhere to tune in. A huge thank you to our incredible partners for making this possible!

All talks are completely new, never before presented at any Living Spaces event, and delivered live throughout the day. We have 18 visionary speakers ready to share insights on the future of architecture, sustainable living, and innovative urban design — including a keynote on the evolving relationship between space, structure, and society.
